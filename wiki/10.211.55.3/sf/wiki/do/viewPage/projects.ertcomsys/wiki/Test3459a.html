wiki1064: Test3 (Version 3)
Test 3 is designed to exercise the error pathways of the core kernel design: tasking, resources, ISRs, events, etc. (it does not cover counters, alarms, schedule tables). It uses four tasks:
  • TaskA is a basic task and autostarted. It runs phase 1 of the test. It kicks off TaskB and TaskC.
  • TaskB is a basic task of lowest priority. It runs phase 3 of the test.
  • TaskC is an extended task. It runs phases 2a and 2c of the test. It activates TaskD and always re-awakes TaskD.
  • TaskD is an extended task. It runs phase 2b of the test.
